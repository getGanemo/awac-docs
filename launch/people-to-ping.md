## Outreach script (DM-friendly, ~3 sentences)

> Hi [name] — saw your [specific recent post / talk / repo]. I just open-sourced a small thing called AWaC that does for AI-agent workspaces what Terraform did for infra: declarative manifest, versioned stacks, deterministic composition. Felt like it was up your alley. Repo: github.com/getGanemo/workspace-cli — would love to hear if it lands or if I missed something obvious.

Customize the first sentence per person. Never paste the same DM twice.

## Anti-patterns

- ❌ Bulk DM scripts.
- ❌ "Hey checking in if you got a chance to look at this" follow-ups.
- ❌ Begging for stars or RTs.
- ❌ Referencing this list publicly. The list is internal only — never post or share it.
- ❌ Offering anything in exchange (compensation, equity, mention) — kills credibility.
